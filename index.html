About Danny

In 2009 Danny Maupin hung up his guitar, quit touring with his band and started performing comedy in his hometown of Louisville, KY before relocating to Chicago. Through years of performing, Danny has developed a unique comedic voice derived from unapologetic self-awareness and quick-witted storytelling.

Always staying busy, Danny can be found co-hosting The Late Late Breakfast at The Hideout, co-producing the popular Kick Punch Stand Up Showcase, performing improv at the world famous iO Theater, showcasing around the Midwest and putting the finishing touches on his self-produced and self-released comedy special, "Future Smiles."

Danny has appeared on The Bob and Tom Show, The Chicago Comedy Exposition of 2014, The Forecastle Music and Arts Festival and shared the stage with Ryan Singer, Tim Northern, Dave Waite, Alex Stone, Tom Brady, Junior Stopka, Adam Burke and Peter Frampton. Yep. Peter Frampton. He definitely came alive.
