A reformed rocker-turned comedian, Danny Maupin has been entertaining audiences for more than half a decade. With his unique style of self-aware wit, southern twang and uncompromising stage presence, Danny can be seen at shows throughout the greater Midwest.

About Danny

In 2009 Danny Maupin hung up his guitar, quit touring with his band and started performing comedy in his hometown of Louisville, KY before relocating to Chicago. Through years of performing, Danny has developed a unique comedic voice derived from personal experiences and unapologetic self-awareness.

Always staying busy, Danny can be found co-hosting The Late Late Breakfast at The Hideout, co-producing the popular Wonder Bar Comedy Showcase, performing improv at the world famous iO Theater, showcasing around the Midwest and putting the finishing touches on his self-produced and self-released comedy special, "Future Smiles."

Danny has appeared on The Bob and Tom Show and shared the stage with Tim Northern, Dave Waite, Raanan Hershberg, Josh Murphy, Tom Brady, Junior Stopka, Adam Burke and Peter Frampton. Yep. Peter Frampton. He definitely came alive.
